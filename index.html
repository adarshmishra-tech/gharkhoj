Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to GharKhoj ("we", "our", or "us"). GharKhoj is a platform that helps users in Nepal discover rooms, hostels, flats, apartments, and shared accommodations.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Android application ("App").

By using GharKhoj, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Name, profile photo, phone number, and email address.
  • Room listing details including title, description, price, amenities, photos, videos, and location.
  • Messages exchanged between users.
  • Saved rooms, reviews, filters, and preferences.
  • Support requests and communications.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Approximate or precise device location for nearby room recommendations and map functionality.
  • Device information such as Android version, device model, IP address, and identifiers.
  • Usage analytics including searches, room views, clicks, swipes, and interactions.
  • Push notification tokens for delivering alerts and updates.

2.3 Media Access

We request access to your photos or videos only when you choose to upload media for room listings or profile updates.

2.4 Third-Party Services

GharKhoj uses trusted third-party services including:

  • Firebase Authentication for account login and security.
  • Firebase Firestore / Database for storing application data.
  • Firebase Storage for uploaded media and listing photos.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging for notifications.
  • Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics for app monitoring.
  • Google Maps for maps and location services.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide room discovery, listings, maps, chat, and recommendation features.
  • Display nearby rooms and calculate distances.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.
  • Send notifications related to messages, room updates, and important activity.
  • Prevent spam, fraud, fake listings, abuse, and policy violations.
  • Maintain platform security and integrity.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing of Information

4.1 Public Listings

Room listings posted on GharKhoj may be publicly visible to other users, including room details, photos, approximate location, price, and associated profile information.

Users should avoid posting sensitive personal information in listings, uploaded media, or chat messages.

4.2 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers that help us operate the App, including hosting, analytics, notifications, and map services.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, legal requests, or to protect users and platform safety.

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred.

We do not sell user personal information to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures, including encrypted connections, secure Firebase infrastructure, and restricted access controls.

However, no online platform can guarantee complete security. Users are responsible for protecting their login credentials and devices.

6. Data Retention

  • We retain information while your account remains active or as necessary to provide services.
  • Listings and uploaded media may remain visible until deleted or removed.
  • Chat history may be retained temporarily for safety and dispute resolution purposes.
  • Location information used for nearby room recommendations is not permanently stored.

Account deletion requests are generally processed within a reasonable period, unless retention is required by applicable law.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access or update your account information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Disable push notifications through device settings.
  • Revoke location permission at any time.
  • Request data deletion through support contact.

8. Fraud Prevention & Platform Safety

GharKhoj may monitor reports, suspicious activities, spam behavior, fake listings, and abusive content to maintain a safe platform for all users.

Users can report listings, profiles, or inappropriate behavior directly through the App.

9. User Responsibility

GharKhoj acts only as a platform connecting tenants, roommates, and property owners. We are not responsible for payments, agreements, disputes, scams, or interactions between users.

Users should verify room details independently and avoid making payments before proper verification.

10. Children's Privacy

GharKhoj is intended for users aged 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed on servers located outside Nepal, including services operated by Google and Firebase infrastructure.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes may be communicated through the App, push notifications, or related communication channels.

Continued use of GharKhoj after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
